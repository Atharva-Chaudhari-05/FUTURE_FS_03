Got Questions? 🤔

Do you offer Jain food options?

Yes! Several of our vegetarian dishes can be prepared without onion and garlic upon request. Just let your server know or add it to the special instructions when ordering online.

Do you deliver to Camp area?

Absolutely. We deliver across Malegaon, including Camp, Soygaon, and Sangmeshwar areas. Delivery is free for orders above ₹300.

Can I book the restaurant for a private party?

Yes, we host birthdays, anniversaries, and corporate events. Please contact us at least 3 days in advance to discuss arrangements and special party menus.
