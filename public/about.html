Our Story 📖

Where passion meets the plate

From a Small Stall to Malegaon's Favorite

In 2010, Suresh Patil started Mirchi & Masala with nothing but a small pushcart, a secret family spice mix, and a dream. What began as a humble street food stall serving only Vada Pav and Misal Pav quickly became the talk of the town.

Word of mouth spread, and by 2015, we opened our first brick-and-mortar restaurant. Today, Mirchi & Masala stands as a testament to the fact that authentic flavors and hard work never go out of style.

We now serve over 500 happy customers every day, offering a menu that perfectly balances the quick thrill of street food with the soul-satisfying comfort of traditional Maharashtra Thalis.
