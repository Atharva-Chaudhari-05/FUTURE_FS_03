Welcome to Spice Garden

A Legacy of Authentic Flavors

What started as a small roadside eatery in 2009 has blossomed into the city's most beloved authentic Indian Dhaba. Our founder, Ramesh Ji, believed in one simple philosophy: serve food that tastes exactly like a mother's home-cooked meal.

We handpick our spices, grind our own masalas, and slow-cook our dishes to preserve the rich heritage of Indian culinary traditions. Every dish that comes out of our kitchen is a tribute to the diverse and vibrant flavors of India.

- Ramesh Ji, Founder

Quality Ingredients

We source 100% organic, farm-fresh vegetables and premium spices daily to ensure the highest quality in every bite.

Authentic Process

No shortcuts. Our Dal Makhani simmers overnight on slow coals, and our breads are baked in traditional clay tandoors.

Heartfelt Service

At our Dhaba, every guest is treated like family. Experience true Indian hospitality with our warm and welcoming staff.
